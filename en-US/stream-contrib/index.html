CentOS Stream Contributor’s Guide

CentOS Stream is a continuously delivered distribution that tracks just ahead of Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) development, positioned as a midstream between Fedora and RHEL. This guide explains the contribution process for CentOS Stream as well as the project’s technical background.

To learn more about CentOS Stream, see the FAQ. Downloads are available on the project page. Composes are available here.

If you wish to contribute to this guide, click the Edit this page link in the top right corner of any page, and open an issue or submit a pull request.
